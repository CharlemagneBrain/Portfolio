About Me

I am a Senegalese PhD student in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Montpellier (INRAE-TETIS), dedicated to leveraging AI across diverse domains. My research focuses on improving food crisis monitoring and detection through spatial information extraction and advanced NLP techniques applied to textual data.

With hands-on experience in AI research and development, I am committed to driving technological innovation for the betterment of society, particularly in addressing critical challenges in food security and healthcare.
